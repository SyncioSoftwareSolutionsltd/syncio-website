Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do I need any special hardware?
No. Restro works on any smartphone, tablet or laptop you already own. Zero hardware investment required.
How long does setup take?
Your dedicated Restro partner configures your entire system within 3 days. You will be ready to go live in under a week.
What if my internet goes down?
Restro keeps working offline. Orders queue automatically when internet drops and sync the moment you reconnect. No lost sales.
Is my restaurant data safe?
Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely in the cloud. You retain full ownership and can export your data anytime.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice by emailing support@synciosolutions.com. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.
What does the 1% transaction fee apply to?
Only to payments processed through Paystack online and QR ordering. Cash and manual payments have no transaction fee.
